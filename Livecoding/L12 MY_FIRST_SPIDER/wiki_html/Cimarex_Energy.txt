</section><h2 class="section-heading" onclick="javascript:mfTempOpenSection(1)"><div class="mw-ui-icon mw-ui-icon-element indicator mw-ui-icon-small mw-ui-icon-flush-left"></div><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><a href="/w/index.php?title=Cimarex_Energy&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History" data-section="1" class="mw-ui-icon mw-ui-icon-element mw-ui-icon-minerva-edit-enabled edit-page mw-ui-icon-flush-right">Edit</a></span></h2><section class="mf-section-1 collapsible-block" id="mf-section-1">
<p>The company was founded in 2002.<sup id="cite_ref-10K_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10K-1">[1]</a></sup> The company was a <a href="/wiki/Corporate_spin-off" title="Corporate spin-off">corporate spin-off</a> of <a href="/wiki/Helmerich_%26_Payne" title="Helmerich &amp; Payne">Helmerich &amp; Payne</a>, which sought to separate its exploration and production business from its drilling business.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> In September 2002, shareholders of <a href="/wiki/Helmerich_%26_Payne" title="Helmerich &amp; Payne">Helmerich &amp; Payne</a> received shares in Cimarex.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p><p>In 2005, the company acquired Magnum Hunter Resources in a $2.1 billion transaction.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p><p>In 2008, the company bought 38,000 acres in Oklahoma from <a href="/wiki/Chesapeake_Energy" title="Chesapeake Energy">Chesapeake Energy</a> for $180 million.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup></p><p>In 2012, the company sold assets in <a href="/wiki/Texas" title="Texas">Texas</a> for $294 million.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup></p><p>In 2014, the company acquired assets in the Cana-Woodford, including 140 billion cubic feet equivalent of proved reserves and 50,000 net acres, in a $249 million transaction.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup></p><p>In 2018, the company sold assets in <a href="/wiki/Ward_County,_Texas" title="Ward County, Texas">Ward County, Texas</a> for $570 million.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup></p>
